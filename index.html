AutoMe

Welcome to AutoMe: your friendly Company Automation Calculator. Here's to make it quick and accurate:

This application has a scope of Finance, HR, Customer Service, Supply Chain, Marketing, IT, Strategies, and Others automation computation for company processes.

Instructions:
First, enter the type of operation, then enter the priority from 1-10 of your company on Priority Points.
Example: An IT tech company should have its IT operations the highest point because they prioritize IT.
Next, hit add operation and add a suboperation for that given operation. Add also how much the suboperation is automated on Automated%.

To ensure AI-human intervention balance:
 Finance - has suboperations with automation rates of 70-90%.
HR - has suboperations with automation rates of 40-60%.
Customer Service - has suboperations with automation rates of 50-70%.
Supply Chain - has suboperations with automation rates of 40-60%.
Marketing - has suboperations with automation rates of 40-60%.
IT - has suboperations with automation rates of 70-90%.
Strategy - has suboperations with automation rates of less than 20%.

*Enter 100 for Workload for standard percentage.
*For better calculations, do not skip an operation (Finance, HR, etc.) and suboperation if it is present in your company. When it is not automated yet, instead add every process and put zero if the whole process is not automated.
